Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit extended abstracts of their original research work. All submissions will undergo a peer-review process. Accepted abstracts will be published in the conference proceedings.

  • Extended abstracts should be 2-4 pages in length
  • Use the official 9ACEE template provided below
  • Submit in DOCX or PDF format
  • Include keywords and author affiliations
