Organization

9th Asian Conference on Earthquake Engineering · Kathmandu, Nepal

About the Organization

The conference is organized by National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Authority (NDRRMA) in collaboration with Nepal Academy of Science and Technology (NAST), Asia Conference on Earthquake Engineering (ACEE), and National Society for Earthquake Technology (NSET).

Host Institution

NSET, in close collaboration with national and public, academic and corporate private sector business entities, serves as the host organization for 9ACEE.
